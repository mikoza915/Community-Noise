ANSI S12.9 Part 4: Outdoor Noise Measurement Standard

Standard: ANSI/ASA S12.9-2013/Part 4 (R2023) - "Quantities and Procedures for Description and Measurement of Environmental Sound - Part 4: Noise Assessment and Prediction of Long-term Community Response"

Key Requirements

  • Measurement at receiving property (not just property line)
  • Multiple weightings: dB(A), dB(C) or dB(Z)
  • 1/3 octave band analysis (31.5 Hz - 8 kHz)
  • Minimum 10-minute continuous measurement
  • Meteorological conditions documented
  • Background noise measurement (source off)
  • Character-based adjustments for tonal, LF, impulsive

Why This Standard Matters

  • International recognized (ISO 1996-1/2 equivalent)
  • Addresses elevated sources explicitly
  • Accounts for character/annoyance factors
  • Used by acoustic consultants worldwide
  • Cited in peer city ordinances
  • Prevents gaming through methodology choice
