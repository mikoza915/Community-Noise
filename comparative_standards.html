What Other Cities Do Better

1. Lower Base Limits: 45 dB(A) (Palo Alto, Mountain View, Menlo Park) vs. San Mateo's 50 dB(A)

2. Character Penalties: +5 dB for tonal, impulsive, or low-frequency content

3. Elevated Structures: Explicit language about measuring at receiving point for elevated sources

4. Frequency Analysis: Some require 1/3 octave band measurements

5. ANSI Standards: Reference to ANSI S12.9 for measurement methodology
