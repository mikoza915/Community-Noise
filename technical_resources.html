Distance & Elevation Effects

Ground-to-Ground

Attenuation: ~6 dB per doubling of distance

Ground absorption: Additional 3-5 dB for soft ground

Barriers effective: Can block line-of-sight

Elevated-to-Ground

Attenuation: ~6 dB per doubling distance

Ground absorption: Minimal (clear path)

Barriers ineffective: Source overhead

Elevated-to-Elevated

Attenuation: Only distance (6 dB/doubling)

Ground absorption: None (airborne path)

Barriers ineffective: Clear line-of-sight
