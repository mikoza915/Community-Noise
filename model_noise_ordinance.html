Model Noise Ordinance: Proposed Improvements to SMMC 7.30

Key Improvements Over Current Ordinance

  • Lower base limit: 45 dB(A) nighttime (from 50 dB(A))
  • Character penalties: +5 dB tonal, +5 dB low-frequency
  • Elevated structures: Explicit measurement requirements
  • ANSI S12.9 Part 4: Standardized methodology
  • Rooftop equipment: Specific mitigation requirements

Â§ 7.30.XXX - Rooftop Equipment Acoustic Mitigation (NEW)

(a) Applicability:
    New or replacement rooftop HVAC equipment on structures within 1,000 feet 
    of residential zoning must demonstrate compliance at receiving properties.

(b) Design Requirements:
    (1) Acoustic enclosures with STC 30+ walls
    (2) Acoustic louvers providing minimum 15 dB insertion loss
    (3) Design by qualified acoustic consultant (PE or INCE Board Certified)
    (4) Account for low-frequency content (31.5-250 Hz) and tonal characteristics

(c) Verification:
    (1) Post-installation measurements at receiving properties
    (2) Before/after comparison demonstrating compliance
    (3) Measurements per ANSI S12.9 Part 4 with character penalties
